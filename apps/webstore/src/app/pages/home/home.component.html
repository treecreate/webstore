Product

Unique wood design with several styling options which make every product become completely unique. In just a few minutes, you can create your own family tree and get it laser-cut into a beautiful piece of oak wood.

Create

There are many ways to create your family tree design. In this example, of a family tree, the couples in the family are connected by having their names placed next to each other. If there are too many names you can also put the couples on top of each other so the tree doesn't get too wide. Be inspired or experiment with your own design, until you find the design that suits you best.

Package

When you purchase a tree design, you are automatically planting a tree. The packaging is made with a zero procent waste agenda: 0% plastic and 100% reused FSC certified paper. You also receive a treecreate keychain, as well as a wooden stand that matches your family tree.
